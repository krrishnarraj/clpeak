Privacy Policy

This application is based on the open-source project "clpeak", a benchmarking tool for OpenCL-capable devices.

Data Collection and Use:
This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

All benchmarking operations are performed locally on the user's device. No data is sent to any external servers.

Permissions:
The app may access device hardware components such as CPU and GPU strictly for benchmarking purposes. These permissions are not used to collect personal information.

Third-party Services:
This app does not use any third-party services (such as analytics, advertising, or cloud services) that collect user data.

Open Source:
The source code for this application is publicly available at:
https://github.com/krrishnarraj/clpeak

Changes to This Privacy Policy:
This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected in this file.
